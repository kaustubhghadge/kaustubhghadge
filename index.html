Param - building a digital magazine in Marathi Language

"Param - meaning "supreme" in Sanskrit, is a digital magazine focused around science, philosophy, society and culture in Marathi language (one of the official languages in India). The idea of our digital magazine is to feed the intellectual curiosity of Marathi population. Especially, taking a step forward in building Marathi speaking intelligentsia. This is a preliminary step in building India an informed democracy which is ready for the future challenges. Our main aim with this venture is to help in sculpting the Indian citizens as a “knowledge workers”. We would like to work with some of the most prominent scientists, sociologists, anthropologists, linguists, philosophers, civic activists, artists, etc. from Maharashtra, India and abroad.


We are determined to measure the power and influence of the society by assessing the culture. We comprehend that religion is not necessarily culture, but it is an aspect of it. The words culture and tradition are often used interchangeably to describe the heritage of India. But everything boils down to religion the minds of Indians, possibly because we’re not able to grasp the complexities of other fields which are intertwined with the culture. We believe culture to be the manifestation of human intellectual achievement.


We believe such in fostering such a culture for India in the near future. We would like to start this in Marathi and later expand to other Indian languages. The goal is to promote – ideas, ideas and ideas! We want to explain complex topics to general Marathi speaking population in layman’s terms. We do not want to lean right, left of the spectrum. We do not wish to publish about politics in state, country, religion, entertainment, sports. These topics are covered in other magazines and daily newspapers. We want our Marathi reader to be informed rather than opinionated."

The MVP for site was launched on 2nd July. We have some prominent Marathi authors contributing to this magazine. We're targetting the first release by September 2017.
